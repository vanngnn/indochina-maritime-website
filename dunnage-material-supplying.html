Service Scope

What We Provide

Reliable material sourcing, delivery coordination, and on-ground support for vessel cargo protection requirements.

Types of cargo we support

  1. Dry bulk and bagged cargo (rice, feed, grains)
  2. General cargo and mixed consignments
  3. Palletized and packaged consumer goods
  4. Project and industrial cargo with special securing needs

What we use

  1. Wooden dunnage, wedges, and packing supports
  2. Lashing belts, wires, clips, and cargo securing tools
  3. Custom quantities based on vessel and cargo plan
  4. Short-notice and urgent supply mobilization
